HCI

Urban Complexity Lab

Projects

Prof. Dr. Patrick Baudisch - The Human Computer Interaction Lab

Definitely worth having a play with the interactives!

Isoscope (http://isoscope-two.fh-potsdam.de/) how far can you travel from a given point by different modalities at different times of the day
Probing projections - errors and issues with arrangements in visualisation (Julian Stahnke)
City Flows (Till Nagel) - mapping bike journeys in a way that people can engage with - designed for exhibition spaces rather than the web
Novel city maps - how do the maps used in novels compare with real maps of those cities (https://uclab.fh-potsdam.de/NCM/Vis/)
Shifted Maps - Heike Otten, Lennard Hildebrand (http://shifted-maps.com/)
Past Visions (https://uclab.fh-potsdam.de/fw4/en/vis/) - really nice technical interface to explore collections, but clear and easy to use
Coin collections - make sure you always have context for where you are in the collection
